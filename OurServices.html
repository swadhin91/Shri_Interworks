Recruitment and Deployment Timeline

We strive for efficient placement while ensuring all legal and logistical requirements are met. Here's a general timeline for our process:

  • **Recruitment Period:** Approximately **6 weeks**, dependent on the quantity and specific qualifications required.
  • **Visa Processing:** Approximately **4-5 months** after the official Work Permit (WP) is secured.
  • **Total Process Duration:** Typically **4-7 months**, varying based on the time taken to obtain the Work Permit.
